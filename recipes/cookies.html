Cookie Recipe

>

Description

This is the best cookie recipe a frind gave me once. So i want to share it with you!

It is simple, easy and has only few ingredients.

Ingredients
  • 250g butter
  • 250g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 350g flour
  • 1 tbsp backing powder
  • 1 tbsp natron
  • 200g chocolate
Steps
  1. Melt butter and mix with sugar
  2. Mix with eggs
  3. Mix with flour, backing powder and natron
  4. Put chocolate in the dough
  5. Bake at 175 degrees for 10-15 minutes
